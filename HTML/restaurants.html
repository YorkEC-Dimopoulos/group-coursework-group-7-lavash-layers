Well-known restaurants in Yerevvan

Yerevan has a a vibrant restaurant scene, combining traditional Armenian eateries with modern, innovative dining spaces. Restaurants are popular for enjoying full meals, sampling local cuisine, and socializing with family and friends.

Sherep

Contemporary Armenian Restaurant

Cuisine type:Armenian, European fusion

Adress:29 Tumanyan Street, Yerevan, Armenia

Short Description A modern Armenian restaurant blending traditional flavors with contemporary presentation. Offers a stylish atmosphere and is popular with locals and tourists alike.

Price Range:AMD 12,000-25,000 per person

Recommended Dishes:

  • Sevan trout
  • Lamb kebab
  • Seasonal Armenian salads

Pandok Yerevan

Family-owned Traditional Armenian Restaurant

Cuisine type:Armenian, Traditional

Adress:12 Teryan, Yerevan, Armenia

Short DescriptionA cozy, family-run restaurant offering authentic Armenian dishes in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Known for its hearty meat dishses and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Price Range8000 Amd- 15000 AMD per person

Recommended Dishes:

  • Khorovats (Armenian barbecue)
  • Dolma
  • Lavash with herbs

Kavkazkaya Plennica

Caucasian & Armenian Cuisine

Cuisine Type:Armenia, Caucasian

Address:7 Abovyan Street, Yerevan, Armenian>

Short Description:A lively restaurant specializing in Caucasian and Armenian dishes. Offers a rustic, traditional interior and generous portions. Known for its grilled meats and festive atmosphere.

Price Range:AMD 10,000-20,000 per person

Recommended Dishes:

  • Khorovats (barbecue)
  • Lamb shashlik
  • Khinkali (dumplings)

Dolmama

Upscale Armenian Restaurant

Cuisine Type:Armenian, Fine dining

Adress:18 Tumanyan Street, Yerevan, Armenia

Short Description:A stylish restaurant offering modern interpretations of traditional Armenian cuisine. Elegant interior and refined dishes make it perfect for special occasions or fine dining experiences.

Price Range: AMD 15,000-30,000 per person

Recommended Dishes:

  • Stuffed grape leaves (Dolma)
  • Grilled lamb
  • Armenian cheese platter

Katsin

Traditional Armenian Dining

Cuisine Type:Armenian, Traditional

Adress:5 Mashtots Avenue, Yerevan, Armenia

Short Description:A traditional Armenian restaurant known for serving classic dishes in a cozy and welcoming environment. Offers a taste of authentic home-style cooking with local ingredients.

Price Range:AMD 7,000-15,000 per person

Recommended Dishes

  • Khash
  • Khorovats
  • Armenian soups and stews

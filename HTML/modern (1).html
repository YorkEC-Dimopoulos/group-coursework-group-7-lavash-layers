Modern Yerevan: Urban Development and Cultural Revival

Yerevan's modern era began after the collapse of the Soviet Union in 1991. The city has experienced a renaissance, with renewed interest in its historical heritage and a focus on urban development and cultural institutions.

Significant efforts have been made to restore and preserve historical sites, while new architectural projects reflect contemporary styles and the city's growing role as a cultural and economic hub in the region.

Modern Yerevan balances its rich historical legacy with the demands of a vibrant, evolving metropolis, showcasing a blend of ancient traditions and modern innovations.

Quick facts

  • Turning point: 1991 independence
  • Theme: Urban Development and Cultural Revival
  • Modern Life: Cafes, Museums and Festivals
  • Cityscape: Modern architecture and cultural institutions

Nightlife in Yerevan

When the Sun Sets, the City Comes Alive

From sophisticated cocktail bars to energetic nightclubs, Yerevan offers unforgettable experiences after dark.

Experience Yerevan After Dark

The nightlife scene in Yerevan is diverse and welcoming. Whether you're looking for live music, craft cocktails, dancing, or just a place to socialize with friends, you'll find it here.

Must-Visit Nightlife Spots

Cascade Lounge & Bar

Upscale rooftop bar with stunning views of the city and creative cocktail menu.

Location: Cascade Complex, Tamanyan Street

Type: Rooftop Bar / Lounge

Vibe: Sophisticated, scenic, perfect for sunset drinks

Price Range: €€€

Hours: 6 PM - 2 AM

C.View Sky Bar

Stunning rooftop venue located on the 13th floor, offering panoramic views of Yerevan and Mount Ararat.

Location: 1 Marshal Baghramyan Ave (Opera Suite Hotel)

Type: Rooftop Cocktail Bar

Vibe: Chic, modern decor, electric atmosphere

Price Range: €€€

Hours: 7 PM - 2 AM

Kami Music Club

Premier musical club renowned for pulsating energy, eclectic music, and live performances.

Location: 18 Abovyan St (near Moscow Cinema)

Type: Music Club / Nightclub

Vibe: Stylish, energetic, dance-focused

Price Range: €€€ (Entry fee often applies)

Hours: 9 PM - 3 AM

Serena Cocktail Bar

Hidden gem inside a historic mansion featuring experimental cocktails and tropical vintage decor.

Location: 3/1 Abovyan St

Type: Speakeasy / Cocktail Bar

Vibe: Vintage, artistic, tropical flair

Price Range: €€€

Hours: 7 PM - 2 AM

Simona Bar

Vibrant bar known for its 'Spirit of the City' philosophy, funk/disco music, and classic cocktails.

Location: 80 Aram Street

Type: Cocktail Bar

Vibe: Energetic, retro funk/80s music, friendly

Price Range: €€

Hours: 6:30 PM - 2 AM

Dargett Craft Beer & Pub

Local favorite serving Armenian craft beers and casual pub atmosphere.

Location: Saryan Street

Type: Pub / Beer Bar

Vibe: Casual, friendly, local crowd

Price Range: €

Hours: 5 PM - 2 AM

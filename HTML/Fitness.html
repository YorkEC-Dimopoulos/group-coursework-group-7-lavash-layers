Sports & Fitness in Yerevan

Stay Active in the Heart of the Caucasus

Yerevan offers countless opportunities to stay fit and active.

Gyms & Fitness Centers

Gold's Gym

International chain with multiple locations in Yerevan, offering world-class equipment and training.

Location: Multiple locations (Kentron, Arabkir)

Hours: 7 AM - 11 PM daily

Orange Fitness

Modern fitness club with comprehensive facilities and professional trainers.

Location: Komitas Avenue, Kentron

Hours: 6 AM - 11 PM daily

GrandSport

Large sports complex with gym, pool, and various sports facilities under one roof.

Location: Davtashen, Northern Avenue area

Hours: 7 AM - 11 PM daily

Cascade Stairs Workout

The iconic Cascade offers 572 steps - perfect for a challenging cardio workout with stunning city views.

Location: Cascade Complex, Tamanyan Street

Cost: Free

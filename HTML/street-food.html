Street Food & Cafes in Yerevan

Gata

Sweet Armenian pastry, often stuffed with nuts and sugar. Sold in bakeries and street markets throughout Yerevan.

Jengyalov Hats

Stuffed flatbread filled with fresh herbs. A popular street snack with locals and tourists.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular street food in Yerevan, made with marinated meat (usually chicken or beef) cooked on a vertical rotisserie, then wrapped in lavash with fresh vegetables and sauces. Perfect for a quick, flavorful meal on the go.

Cafes in Yerevan

Yerevan has a growing cafe culture. Many cafes combine traditional Armenian teas and coffee with modern interiors. They are perfect for relaxing or meeting friends.

Sorriso Cafe

Sorriso Café is a modern café in Yerevan inspired by Italian coffee culture. It is known for its high-quality espresso, fresh pastries, and relaxed atmosphere. Sorriso is a popular place for casual meetings, short breaks, and enjoying coffee in the city center.

Santa Fe

Santa Fe Café is a well-known café chain in Yerevan that combines international coffee styles with a welcoming atmosphere. It is especially popular for its wide menu of coffees, desserts, and breakfast options, making it suitable for both social gatherings and everyday visits.

Tips for Tourists

  • Language: Armenian is the official language. English is commonly spoken among younger people and in tourist areas, but learning a few basic Armenian phrases is appreciated.
    • Hello:"Barev"
    • Thank you:"Shnorhakalutyun"
    • Please:"Xndrum em"
    • Goodbye:"Ctesutyun"
  • Accepting Offers Armenian is the official language.Armenians take pride in offering food to guests. It is polite to accept at least a small portion, as refusing may be seen as impolite.
  • Sharing Meals: Meals are often social occasions. Take your time to enjoy food and conversation with your hosts or companions.
  • Takeaway and Delivery: If you prefer to enjoy meals at your accommodation or on the go, there are many delivery apps available in Yerevan, such as Glovo, that bring food directly to your location.

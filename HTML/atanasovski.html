History of Yerevan

This website presents Yerevan's history in 4 major time periods: Erebuni with ancient origins, medieval and early development, Soviet era transformation and the post 1991 period after gaining independence.

Timeline of Yerevan's History

A short timeline for quick reference

Major Events in Yerevan's History
Period Date / Century Key Events
Ancient Yerevan 782 BC - 4th Century AD Founding of Erebuni Fortress, incorporation into various empires including Urartian, Persian, and Roman.
Medieval Yerevan 5th Century - 18th Century Development under Byzantine, Arab, Seljuk, Mongol, and Ottoman rule; establishment as a regional center.
Soviet Yerevan 1920 - 1991 Transformation into a Soviet city; industrialization and urban development; cultural renaissance.
Modern Yerevan 1991 - Present Independence from the Soviet Union; modernization efforts; growth as a cultural and economic hub.

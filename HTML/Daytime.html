Daytime in Yerevan

Discover the City's Vibrant Coffee Culture

Start your day with exceptional coffee and views.

Must-Visit Daytime Spots

The Cascade Cafe

Located at the base of the iconic Cascade complex, this cafe offers stunning views and excellent coffee.

Location: Tamanyan Street, Kentron

Vibe: Artistic, scenic, perfect for morning coffee

Must Try: Armenian coffee with gata (traditional pastry)

Republic Square Terrace

Experience the heart of Yerevan with a cafe right on the main square, surrounded by pink tuff architecture.

Location: Republic Square, Kentron

Vibe: Historic, central, people-watching paradise

Must Try: Iced coffee with a view of the dancing fountains

Northern Avenue Brew

Modern specialty coffee shop with expertly crafted espresso drinks and a minimalist aesthetic.

Location: Northern Avenue, Kentron

Vibe: Modern, trendy, perfect for remote work

Must Try: Pour-over coffee with local pastries

Pet-Friendly Yerevan

Exploring the City with Your Furry Friends

Yerevan is becoming increasingly welcoming to pets, especially dogs.

Parks & Green Spaces for Pets

Lovers Park

Beautiful landscaped park in Arabkir district with shaded paths, fountains, and green lawns perfect for leisurely walks.

Circular Park (Swan Lake)

Central park surrounding Swan Lake with 2.5km loop, perfect for daily dog walks in the heart of the city.

Pet-Friendly Cafes & Restaurants

In Vino

Wine bar with outdoor seating that welcomes dogs. Popular evening spot for pet owners.

The Green Bean

Coffee shop with garden seating area where pets are welcome.

Dolmama Restaurant

Traditional Armenian restaurant with courtyard that accepts well-behaved dogs.

Veterinary Clinics

Vet Center Yerevan

Modern veterinary clinic with comprehensive services and emergency care.
